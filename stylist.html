The Science of Color

Why the right colors change everything

Personal color analysis is the science of identifying which colors — based on your unique skin undertone, hair pigment, and eye color — make you look your most vital, healthy, and confident. The right palette can make your complexion appear clearer, your eyes brighter, and your overall presence more commanding.

Conversely, wearing the wrong colors can cause your complexion to appear sallow, your face to look tired, and even add apparent weight. Color analysis removes the guesswork — so every item you wear works for you.

The 16-Season System

Your unique season, perfectly defined

The 16-season method expands on the classic 4-season model to provide a far more precise color palette — identifying not just your primary season but its sub-flow for a truly personalised result.

Spring

Warm, clear, and light. Peachy nudes, coral, warm yellows, and fresh greens.

Summer

Cool, muted, and soft. Dusty roses, lavenders, soft blues, and powder tones.

Autumn

Warm, muted, and deep. Burnt orange, olive, terracotta, and rich earth tones.

Winter

Cool, clear, and intense. True white, black, deep jewel tones, and icy brights.
