Yoga & Aerial Yoga

Ground yourself. Then take flight.

Traditional yoga practice weaves physical postures (Asanas) with breathwork (Pranayama) and meditation to promote flexibility, strength, balance, and deep body awareness. Aerial yoga adds a suspended dimension — enabling deeper spinal decompression, inversions, and core engagement that floor-based yoga cannot achieve.

Golf Pilates

A longer drive starts with a stronger core.

Golf Pilates targets the deep stabilizers of the abdomen, back, and hips that are essential for a consistent, powerful swing. By correcting muscle imbalances and improving rotational flexibility across the shoulders, hips, and spine, sessions help golfers achieve greater swing amplitude while dramatically reducing the risk of lower back and rotator cuff injuries.

Pregnancy Training

Strong mother, healthy baby.

Specially designed prenatal exercise strengthens the pelvic floor, abdominals, and back muscles used during labour — reducing complications and supporting a smoother delivery. Maintaining cardiovascular fitness and flexibility during pregnancy benefits both mother and developing baby, while targeted exercises counteract the postural shifts that occur through each trimester. Hani also holds Prenatal/Postpartum Pilates instructor certification.

Pelvic Floor Strength Labour Preparation Postural Correction Stress Reduction Postpartum Recovery

Specialised Services

Training adapted to every need

05

Specific Training

For clients with neurological conditions, joint limitations, or rehabilitation needs. Exercises and equipment are carefully adapted to accommodate specific physical constraints while improving functional movement and quality of life. TRX, battle ropes, balance boards, and resistance training tools are available.

06

Deep Tissue Massage

Using slow, deep strokes and targeted pressure to reach deeper muscle layers and connective tissue, deep tissue massage relieves chronic pain, reduces stiffness and adhesions, improves blood circulation, and accelerates injury recovery. Particularly beneficial for repetitive strain, osteoarthritis, fibromyalgia, and postural problems.
